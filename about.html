About Me

Wassup Niggas, I’m Tammy Arnold. Music has always been my passion, and through my songs I share my story, my experiences, and my vision with the world.

Over the years, I have grown from performing in small concerts to bigger tours and collaborations. My journey is defined by persistence, creativity, and love for sound.

Experience

  • 2019: First live performance at a local concert with 500+ audience.
  • 2021: Released debut EP that gained over 50k streams worldwide.
  • 2022: Collaborated with international producers and artists.
  • 2023: Headlined a regional tour across 5 major cities.
  • 2024: Won best Rapper at Grammy Awards.
